CALMANA

Calmana harnesses hundreds of years of climate system learnings to allow you to read the future of water through an ambient augmented reality.

You begin with a reading.

You must find these artefacts in your environment.

These artefacts have been placed by our inhabitants, to inform you of events—both extreme and mundane—yet to happen.

Search the surroundings for what may be out of place, what may stand out, and something may appear.

Carry a card of the artefact and see the future it has foretold.

Combine your carried artefacts with others' for the most accurate depiction of the events soon to occur.
